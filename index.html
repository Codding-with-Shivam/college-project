Ras Malai

Ras Malai is a much-loved, delicious Indian dessert of West Bengali origin made from white cream, sugar, milk, and cardamom-flavored paneer cheese known as chhana. Cashews, almonds and saffron are often added.

Pakora

Pakora is a savory, deep-fried Indian snack made with chunks of vegetables such as potato, cauliflower and eggplant, or meat of choice, which is then dipped in chickpea flour, seasoned with turmeric, salt, chili, or other spices, and deep-fried in ghee.

Chaat

The name chaat encompasses a wide variety of Indian street foods, snacks, or small meals which usually combine salty, spicy, sweet, and sour flavors.

Idli

Idli is a traditional, savory Indian cake that is a popular breakfast item in many South Indian households, although it can be found throughout the country.

Paratha

Paratha is a flaky, layered, golden-brown Indian bread, which is typically consumed for breakfast. It consists of whole wheat flour thatâ€™s baked in ghee, Indian clarified butter, and comes in round, triangular, square, or hexagonal shapes.

Naan

Naan is a well-liked flatbread with a chewy texture. It consists of white flour, yeast, eggs, milk, salt, and sugar, baked in a tandoor oven.

Aloo Gobi

Aloo Gobi is a vegetarian dry curry, consisting of potatoes (aloo), cauliflower (gobi), and Indian spices. It has a warm, yellow-orange color because it contains turmeric.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a dessert based on milk solids that are kneaded into a dough, shaped into balls, and deep-fried in ghee.

Sahi paneer

Kulafi

Kulfi is a classic Indian ice cream made with slowly simmered whole milk. Although the long-simmering process results in a loss of volume, it makes up for it with a delicious, nutty, caramelized flavor.

Sambar

Sambar is a tamarind-based broth, prepared with lentils and vegetables. It originates from Tamil Nadu, but it is also very popular throughout South India.

Dal makhni

The name makhani, meaning butter, is due to the addition of cream and a drizzle of melted ghee or butter that provides the typical velvety flavor of this classic.
